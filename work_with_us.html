It's a Very Simple Process

  • Submit the property’s characteristics.

  • We match your seller with one or multiple of our investors.

  • Receive cash offers and rental terms

  • Your client cashes out, and stays as a renter while you get paid your commission, just like in any other sale

How do we make it possible?

We Want What’s Best For The Homeowner

We don’t purchase homes for ourselves. We aim to create competition between multiple buyers in order to optimize the sales price for the homeowner. We truly believe some sellers prefer to stay in their own homes after cashing out. We facilitate the negotiations for the leaseback before the sale. Giving the owner peace of mind.

You Are Always In Control Of Your Deals

We will use your contract and stay behind the scenes. The realtor will be the point of contact with the seller. We will communicate with the buyer

We Work With The Best Players In The Market

TWe have experienced professionals working with seasoned investors. Our investors range from the biggest SFR Funds in the US to top local investors. Our technology makes it easy to quickly review assets that match your unique buy box.

You can purchase the themes on Bootstrap Themes via any major credit/debit card (via Stripe) or with your Paypal account. We don't support cryptocurrencies or invoicing at this time.
